Normalisation of insecticide resistance values

Mortality rate
The colouring of percent mortality values has been inspired by the WHO test procedures of Insecticide resistance.
LC50, LC90, LC95, LT50, LT90, LT95 etc
Lethal concentration and lethal time values have very skewed distributions. These are log-transformed and the top and bottom 2% values are discarded before colour scale assignment between the minimum and maximum.
